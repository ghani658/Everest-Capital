Alexander is a Financial Advisor for the Everest Capital Private Wealth Management Group of Wells Fargo Advisors. Prior to joining Wells Fargo Advisors in 2019, Alex served as an intern and client associate for six years before transitioning into the role of Financial Advisor in 2018. Alexander graduated from Brigham Young University – Idaho with a bachelor’s degree in business and finance and has taken advanced modern finance coursework through the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He has been married to his wife, Kimberly, for six years and resides in Westwood, CA. They enjoy photography, traveling, and serving in the community.
